About

Stop by and listen up at Brown's Record Store, located at 123 Dundas St. London Ontario. We are an independent, locally owned record shop. We have a huge collection of records to choose from so come see what we have in store or check our Products section to see if we have what you are looking for. We also do custom orders, so if you can't find what you must have, let us know and we can order it for you!

Our Team

Colin Brown, Owner

Meet our owner Colin. He's loved collecting records since he was a kid and has a collection of over 1000 records. He loves to talk about music, so if you see him, ask him for music recommendations.

Mike Durham, Merchandise Manager

Mike is our manager of merchandise. If you want to make a custom order, he is your man. You can usually find him at the back of the store at the custom order desk.

Anna Smith, Cashier

Get to know Anna at the cash register. Don't worry, she won't make sure of your music taste. If you can't decide what to get, she will help you decide.
